Description

Get rid of dark spots and uneven skin tone with our 15% Vitamin C Brightening Serum. This powerful serum fights free radicals, reduces inflammation, and evens out your complexion.

This formula contains a stable formulation of ascorbic acid, ferulic acid, vitamin E, and sorbitol. Its silky, non-sticky, and fast-absorbing texture makes it perfect for all skin types.

Benefits

  • Brightens Complexion

  • Balances and evens out skin tone

  • Reduces dark spots and hyperpigmentation

  • Prevents fine lines and wrinkles

How to Use

  • Cleanse and dry your skin.

  • Gently pat the serum onto your face.

  • Use daily in the morning, followed by moisturizer and sunscreen.

  • Avoid direct sunlight and always patch test before use.

Ingredients

Deionized Water, Disodium EDTA, Glycerin, Propylene Glycol, Butylene Glycol, Sorbitol, 2,4 dihydroxy-N-(3-hydroxy propyl)-3,3-dimethylbutanamide, 3-o-Ethyl Ascorbic Acid, Ferulic Acid, Wheat Germ Oil(As Contained Natural Vitamin E), Butylated Hydroxytoluene, DMDM Hydantoin.
