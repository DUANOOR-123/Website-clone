Description

Our Hydrating Cleanser effectively removes makeup, dirt, and debris while preserving your skin’s natural oils and protective barrier. Enriched with hyaluronic acid, ceramides, aloe vera extract, and vitamin E, this formula locks in moisture for soft, smooth, and hydrated skin.

Key Ingredients

  • Hyaluronic Acid: Locks in moisture for hydration.
  • Ceramides: Strengthens the skin barrier.
  • Aloe Vera Extract: Soothes and nourishes the skin.
  • Vitamin E: Provides antioxidant protection.

Benefits

  • Gently removes makeup, dirt, and debris

  • Protects and maintains the skin’s moisture barrier

  • Hydrates and nourishes the skin

How to Use

  • Dispense a pea-sized amount into wet palms.

  • Rub hands to create a lather and massage gently onto wet skin for one minute.

  • Rinse with lukewarm water.

  • Patch test before use and avoid direct sunlight.

Ingredients

Deionized Water, Glycerin, Disodium EDTA, Carbomer, Xanthan Gum, Decyl Glucoside, Propylene Glycol, Butylene Glycol, PEG-100 Stearate, Cetyl Alcohol, Cetostearyl Alcohol, Glyceryl Stearate, Ceramide III, Sodium Lactate, Aloe Vera Extract, Sodium Hyaluronate, Sodium Pyroglutamate, Sodium PCA, Wheat Germ Oil (As Contained Natural Vitamin E), Sodium Citrate, Citric Acid, Triethanolamine, Phenoxyethanol, Caprylyl Glycol, Fragrance.
